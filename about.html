About Us

Food Factory serves Italian, American and Mexican food with a focus on fresh and local ingredients. The concept is brought to provide delicious comfort food in a cozy intimate setting with a unique Ambience

Food Factory, the first waiter-less order taking restaurant in Auckland, allows you to place your order through a conveyor belt made with bike chains.

Driven by Passion, Innovation & Service as our motto, you will experience only the best Food, Music, Ambience and Drinks here!

Food Factory is not only a place to find the best food but a place of enjoyment, excitement for each of our guests. We aim to ensure everyone gets an unforgettable experience!
